Oyster mushrooms in your garden beds

Part of our Mushroom Workshop Series

Learn how to get permanent oyster mushroom patches established in your garden beds using our soil-based mycelium inoculant.

We have developed a method using coconut fibre, perlite, sawdust and maize that can be dug into your garden beds and produce ongoing crops of oyster mushrooms. By regularly feeding the beds with organic material, you will be able to maintain the production of mushrooms all year round.
